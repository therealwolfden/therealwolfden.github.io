Below is a calculator that can be used to calculate your final grade in the class based on the Fall 2022 syllabus. To use the calculator enter your current percentage grades into their corresponding number boxes. You may enter the total grade for each section to calculate your final grade. Leaving a grade blank and entering a value into the final grade section will calculate what you need to make on that section to reach that desired grade. If multiple assignments are left blank or if the final grade option is left blank all empty grades will be counted as a zero.
